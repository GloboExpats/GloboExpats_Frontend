### **"Account verification required" message**

**Solution**: 
1. Go to `/account/verification`
2. Verify your email following Step 2 above
3. Refresh the page

---

### **OTP not received**

**Solution**:
1. Check your spam folder
2. Wait 2-3 minutes
3. Click "Send OTP" again
4. Make sure email is correct

---

### **"Invalid OTP" error**

**Solution**:
1. Make sure you entered the code correctly
2. OTP expires after 10 minutes
3. Request a new OTP if expired

---

### **Cart not working after verification**

**Solution**:
1. Log out completely
2. Log back in
3. Try accessing cart again
4. If still not working, contact support
